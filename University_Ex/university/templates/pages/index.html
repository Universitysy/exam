Assignments

The "Assignments" section is where you can view and manage the assignments of teachers to exam rooms. Each row of the table displays the teacher's name, the assigned room number, and the time of the exam. You can easily add a new assignment by clicking on the "Add Assignment" button. This section allows you to keep track of all exam assignments in one place, ensuring that all teachers are properly assigned to their respective rooms and times.
