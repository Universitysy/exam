Welcome to the Examination Supervision Management Website

This website is designed to help you manage and organize the examination supervision at your university.

  • Enter teachers' preferences
  • Enter room capacities
  • Assign teachers to rooms
  • Present teachers' assignments in the form of an excel sheet and a pdf file
  • The possibility to make exchanges between teachers
  • Notify teachers and students before the exam starting
How to use

To use this website, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to the website using your username and password.
  2. Enter the teachers' preferences for the upcoming exams.
  3. Enter the room capacities for each exam room.
  4. Assign teachers to rooms.
  5. Present teachers' assignments in the form of an excel sheet and a pdf file.
  6. Make exchanges between teachers if necessary.
  7. Notify teachers and students before the exam starting.

If you have any questions, please contact the website administrator at administrator@example.com.
